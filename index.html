Why The Euro Hub?

The European Pavilion is more than just an exhibit space; it provides a collaborative environment to share ideas and make deals. It is where European partners come together under one umbrella of European innovations. This year we will select 8-12 partners to be part of our group. By being part of a larger whole, they strengthen each other’s message, help each other on the trade show floor and maximize visibility from attendees. We offer opportunities to make useful introductions by carefully connecting European businesses to our local network. As an innovations partner, World of Walas has been awarded a prime section on the tradeshow floor, total square footage of 1400 sq. ft (130 sq. m) to connect European knowledge and tools to local partners and local demand.

Why Buildex Vancouver?

World of Walas is proud to be the official Education Partner of Buildex 2019!

Now in its 30th year, Buildex Vancouver is Western Canada’s largest tradeshow and conference for the Construction, Renovation, Architecture, Interior Design and Property Management industries. It attracts 600 exhibitors and over 14,000 attendees offering products, materials, services and technologies from more than a hundred different categories. With a goal of providing industry practitioners with the resources and tools they need to grow their knowledge, enhance their practice and advance the industry, BUILDEX Vancouver serves an important role for the this community.

World of Walas

World of Walas is a sustainable urban developer that operates on the West and East Coasts of Canada, the Netherlands, Germany and Costa Rica. Dudoc Vancouver is Walas’ innovation hub that is open to the public and displays industry-leading innovations from our selected partners. They all share a common goal of setting new standards for healthy and energy-efficient communities. The products and projects presented at Dudoc have been implemented in prestigious developments in Vancouver and beyond. We opened in 2014 as a hub for the local community of change makers, builders, architects, designers, aca­demics and more. Our community thrives with ongoing networking events, workshops, well-attended trade missions, conferences and more. Our international network is growing rapidly, with top innovative partners from Europe and beyond.
